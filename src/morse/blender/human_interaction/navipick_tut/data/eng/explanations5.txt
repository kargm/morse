Your next task is to put the cup into the cupboard. 

Press <X> to change your view to the 
first-person perspective.
You can change it back to the third-person perspective
by pressing the key <X> again.

For picking up objects, opening doors and 
drawers as well as operating switches you  
have to be in the first-person mode.

With the third-person perspective you have
a better overview of the environment of your 
avatar. It is recommended for navigation tasks.
